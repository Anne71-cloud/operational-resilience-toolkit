# Regulatory Mapping — Operational Resilience Toolkit

## Overview

This document maps toolkit capabilities to regulatory requirements across
four key operational resilience frameworks applicable to financial institutions.

## Framework Coverage

### DORA — Digital Operational Resilience Act (EU)

| Article | Requirement | Toolkit Module |
|---|---|---|
| Art. 5-16 | ICT Risk Management Framework | Dependency Mapper, Impact Tolerance Engine |
| Art. 8-9 | Identification & Classification | Dependency Mapper (critical functions, dependency chains) |
| Art. 11 | Impact Tolerances | Impact Tolerance Engine |
| Art. 17-23 | ICT Incident Reporting | Incident Tracker |
| Art. 24-27 | Digital Operational Resilience Testing | Scenario Testing Framework |
| Art. 26-27 | Threat-Led Penetration Testing | Scenario Testing (cyber scenarios) |
| Art. 28-44 | ICT Third-Party Risk | Dependency Mapper (third-party analysis) |
| Art. 45 | Information Sharing | Incident Tracker (lessons learned) |

### FINMA — Circular 2023/1 (Switzerland)

| Circular Margin | Requirement | Toolkit Module |
|---|---|---|
| Cm 10-20 | Governance & Organisation | Resilience Scorer (governance pillar) |
| Cm 31-35 | Critical Function Identification | Dependency Mapper |
| Cm 36-40 | Dependency Mapping | Dependency Mapper (end-to-end chains) |
| Cm 41-45 | Impact Tolerances | Impact Tolerance Engine |
| Cm 50-55 | Scenario Testing | Scenario Testing Framework |
| Cm 60-65 | Incident Management | Incident Tracker |
| Cm 70-75 | Third-Party Management | Dependency Mapper (concentration risk) |

| Section | Requirement | Toolkit Module |
